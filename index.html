  • Click the Copy Signature button above — it copies the formatted signature, ready to paste.
  • Open Apple Mail → Mail → Settings → Signatures.
  • Create a new signature, then paste (Cmd+V) into the right pane.
  • Uncheck "Always match my default message font" so the formatting is preserved.
  • Assign the signature to your account.
